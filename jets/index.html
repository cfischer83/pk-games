HOW TO PLAY

Survive 90 seconds over the city. You auto-fly forward. Dodge buildings & hills, dodge enemy fire, and blast the red jets. You can take 3 hits.

KEYBOARD

  • ↑ ↓ ← → / WASD — fly & change altitude
  • SPACE — fire (hold for auto)
  • ENTER — drop smart bomb (×3)
  • P — pause

GAMEPAD

  • Stick / D-Pad — fly
  • A / RT — fire
  • B / X — smart bomb
  • Start — pause

TOUCH

  • Left pad — fly & altitude
  • FIRE — hold to shoot
  • BOMB — smart bomb
  • ❚❚ — pause

Altitude matters: tall buildings can only be cleared by flying high — your shadow on the ground shows how low you are.
